About Dark Tower

Dark Tower was concieved, written, recorded in one weekend and eventually went on to win the Berlin Mini Games Jam event at Game Academy in June 2013.

The force behind it are husband and wife creative team Pete Haughie and Astrid Bin.

The technology used was the fantastic js library Buzz by Jay Salvat, the ubiquitous jQuery for ease of deployment and speed of development, Audacity for recording and Twine for writing and checking that the story all fitted together properly.

Notes: Due to the intensive audio usage it might not work too well on mobile devices and tablets.
